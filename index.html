Hamdi Kavak

I am a PhD Candidate in the department of Modeling, Simulation and Visualization Engineering at Old Dominion University, Virginia USA. My primary research combines big data, agent-based simulation, and machine learning to address problems that are challenging to tackle by each domain independently. I simply call this area data-driven agent-based simulations (DD-ABS). Currently, I apply DD-ABS in studies of human mobility and cybersecurity areas. I am working in my doctoral advisor Dr. Padilla's Human Dynamics Lab at VMASC . See my research and publications.
