I design and develop computational models to study human behavior from the areas of cybersecurity and urban systems. My main research tools are data analytics and agent-based simulation. I occasionally use network science approaches based on the problem.

I work as a Post-Doc/Research Associate at George Mason University's Geography and Geoinformation Science Department. Here, I generally work on designing and developing large-scale computational models to study the behavior of populations. Specifically, I focus on conceptual modeling and Verification&Validation aspects of the models. See my research and publications.
